How Trust Orbit Works

📝 Record Charges

Create charges when someone owes you money. Track who owes what with complete transparency and clarity.

💰 Confirm Payments

Mark payments as paid and let the other person confirm receipt. Both sides must agree before it counts.

⭐ Build Your Score

Your trust score grows with confirmed payments. Show your friends you're reliable and trustworthy.

👥 Social Network

Connect with friends and family. See who you've shared expenses with and track group transactions.

📊 Payment Methods

Store your payment details (SINPE, bank account) so others know exactly how to pay you back.

🔐 Private & Secure

Your data is encrypted and private. We don't touch any money—payments happen between you and your friends.
