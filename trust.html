How it works

Three steps to squared accounts.

The app keeps the ledger honest. You keep control of the money.

01

Record a charge

Log who owes what, for what, and how much, split between friends, roommates, or a group.

02

Pay outside the app

Settle up however you already do: cash, bank transfer, SINPE. Trust Orbit never touches the money.

03

Confirm together

Both sides confirm the payment happened. Only then does it count toward your trust score.

Security & privacy

We never touch your money.

Trust Orbit is a ledger, not a bank. Here's exactly what that means.

No money moves through the app

You settle up directly with your counterparty: cash, bank transfer, or SINPE.

You control visibility

Your trust score is visible to friends by default. Public profile is opt-in, off by default.

No biometric data collected

Face ID and fingerprint app-lock happen entirely on your device. We never see it.

We don't sell your data

No ad targeting, no data brokers. Ever.
