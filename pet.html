The tag

A tag worth showing off.

Stainless steel, laser-engraved QR, and a passive NFC chip. Premium enough for their collar, tough enough for every adventure.

  • No battery, ever. Passive NFC, nothing to charge or replace.
  • Tap or scan. Works on any modern phone, no app required for the finder.
  • Update anytime. Change your number or vet without re-engraving anything.
